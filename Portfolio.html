profile

I'm a software engineer who likes writing things that are small, sturdy, and slightly clever. Most of my best ideas arrive between the second and third coffee, and the rest show up while I'm trying to explain something to a friend who only half-listens.

I learn out loud, ship faster than I plan, and treat unfamiliar codebases the way I treat unfamiliar cities — wander first, take notes, ask the locals. Curiosity is the only stack I'm fully fluent in.
